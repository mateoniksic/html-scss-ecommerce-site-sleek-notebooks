Can I make changes to existing order?

Unfortunately, we can’t make changes or cancel an order after it’s been submitted.

An item may only be canceled if it is on pre-order. If you would like to cancel a pre-ordered item you can visit our contact us page and submit a form.

How long will it take for my order to arrive?

You will receive your notebook within 3 to 5 days via express shipping. The shipping is free of charge.

When will I receive my refund?

If returning by mail, you should receive your refund upon once we receive a notebook and check if it’s still in a good condition.

Please note, it can take 3-5 business days for a refund to credit back to your original form of payment.

Is tax included in the final price?

We unfortunately don't have control over the taxes that are charged - they are added in automatically and are subject to tax laws that are constantly being updated.

Will I be charged right away when I place an order?

Yes, your card will be charged right away. We don't store payment information for security purposes.

What is your return and exchange policy?

All notebooks are guaranteed for 30 days after purchase date. If any issues arise during that time, they can be returned for a refund or exchange.
