Our story

Home / Our story
Rijeka, Croatia (Headquarters)

A true creative professional, Jhon Wick, CEO and founder of Sleek, has always attented meetings armed with a notebook.

Part of his job was designing and developing complex applications, attending online meetings and in his free time he would use his notebook for gaming. He realized that there are not many notebooks that can do both and do it good.

So, he asked himself why – don’t I build a notebook that will merge both of these worlds into one. A perfect notebook that is thin and light to carry, powerful enough to handle all tasks any creative professional would need, and provide immersive gaming experience.

So, in 2021 Jhon, together with Sleek team, set out to create a device that would offer extraordinary performance and portability with long lasting battery. The first notebook that can handle both creative work and gaming while being easy to carry and battery that can be charged super fast and last two times longer than any current market notebook.

The company was founded in Rijeka, Croatia. For more information on how to get in touch with us visit contact us page.
